VUGA TV Platform

Production-ready white-label OTT streaming solution serving the $350B+ global streaming market.

Your Complete OTT Solution

VUGA TV Platform is a comprehensive, production-ready white-label streaming solution designed for content providers, broadcasters, and media companies. Our platform enables you to launch and monetize your own streaming service with native applications across all major devices.

Built on enterprise-grade infrastructure, the platform includes everything you need: native iOS, Android, and TV applications, a powerful content management system, flexible monetization options, real-time analytics, multi-language support, and robust DRM security.

Currently in development: OTT social network integration that will revolutionize how viewers engage with streaming content, creating community-driven viewing experiences.

Platform Capabilities

Everything you need to launch and scale your streaming service.

APP

Native Applications

iOS, Android, Smart TV, Roku, Apple TV, and Amazon Fire TV apps with consistent user experience across all platforms.

Content Management

Powerful CMS with content scheduling, metadata management, category organization, and automated publishing workflows.

Flexible revenue models including SVOD (subscription), AVOD (advertising), TVOD (transactional), and PPV (pay-per-view).

Full localization support with English, Russian, and Uzbek languages built-in. Additional languages available.
